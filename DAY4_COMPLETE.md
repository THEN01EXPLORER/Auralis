## Troubleshooting Guides

### Recording Issues
- Audio too quiet → Increase mic gain
- Video laggy → Close other apps, reduce quality
- Backend not responding → Restart servers
- Can't remember script → Record in segments

### Editing Issues
- Video won't export → Lower quality settings
- Audio out of sync → Re-import, check frame rate
- File too large → Reduce bitrate
- Video pixelated → Increase bitrate
