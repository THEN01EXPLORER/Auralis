## Editing Workflow

### Step 1: Import Raw Footage
1. Open your editing software
2. Create new project: "Auralis Demo Video"
3. Import your best recording take
4. Drag video to timeline

### Step 2: Trim Beginning and End
1. Find the exact moment you start speaking
2. Cut 1-2 seconds before that
3. Find the exact moment you finish
4. Cut 1-2 seconds after that
5. **Goal:** Clean start and end, no dead air

### Step 3: Remove Mistakes (Optional)
If you made mistakes during recording:
1. Find the mistake in timeline
2. Cut before the mistake
3. Cut after you recover
4. Delete the middle section
5. Smooth the transition with a quick fade

### Step 6: Add Zoom/Highlight Effects (Optional)

**When to use:**
- Highlighting small UI elements (badges, confidence scores)
- Drawing attention to specific vulnerabilities
- Showing tab navigation

**How to add:**
1. Find the moment in timeline
2. Add keyframe at start
3. Add keyframe at end
4. Increase scale between keyframes (105-110%)
5. Keep duration short (1-2 seconds)

### Step 7: Add Background Music (Optional)

**Music Selection:**
- Subtle, non-distracting
- Tech/corporate style
- No lyrics
- Royalty-free

**Free Music Sources:**
- YouTube Audio Library
- Incompetech (Kevin MacLeod)
- Bensound
- Free Music Archive

**How to add:**
1. Import music file
2. Drag to audio track below video
3. Reduce volume to 10-20% (very quiet)
4. Fade in at start (2 seconds)
5. Fade out at end (2 seconds)
6. Ensure voice is always clearly audible

**‚ö†Ô∏è Important:** Music should be barely noticeable, just filling silence

### Step 9: Color Correction (Optional)

If video looks washed out or too dark:
1. Adjust brightness (+5 to +10)
2. Adjust contrast (+5 to +10)
3. Adjust saturation (+5 to +10)
4. Keep changes subtle

### Step 10: Audio Enhancement

**Normalize Audio:**
- Ensures consistent volume throughout
- Most editors: Audio ‚Üí Normalize

**Remove Background Noise:**
- If available, use noise reduction filter
- Be subtle, don't make voice sound robotic

**Adjust Volume:**
- Voice should be clear and prominent
- Background music should be barely audible
- No clipping (red peaks in audio meter)

## Export Settings

### Recommended Settings
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Bitrate:** 8-10 Mbps (high quality)
- **Audio:** AAC, 192kbps, 44.1kHz

### Export Presets by Software

**DaVinci Resolve:**
- Deliver ‚Üí YouTube 1080p preset

**Shotcut:**
- Export ‚Üí YouTube preset

**Windows Video Editor:**
- Export ‚Üí High 1080p

**Clipchamp:**
- Export ‚Üí 1080p

### File Size
- Target: 100-300 MB for 3 minutes
- If too large: Reduce bitrate to 6-8 Mbps
- If too small: Increase bitrate to 10-12 Mbps

## Advanced Editing Tips (Optional)

### Smooth Transitions
- Use 0.5-1 second crossfades between cuts
- Avoid jarring jump cuts
- Match audio levels across cuts

### Cursor Highlighting
- Add a colored circle around cursor for important clicks
- Use sparingly, only for key actions

### Picture-in-Picture
- Show architecture diagram in corner while narrating
- Show AWS logo during "Built on AWS" section

### Captions/Subtitles
- Add for accessibility
- Use auto-caption tools (YouTube, Kapwing)
- Review and correct any errors

### B-Roll Inserts
- Cut away to screenshots of news articles during hook
- Show AWS Bedrock logo during AI explanation
- Keep inserts brief (2-3 seconds)

## Common Editing Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå **Over-editing:** Too many effects, distracting
‚ùå **Music too loud:** Drowns out narration
‚ùå **Text too small:** Unreadable on mobile
‚ùå **Text too long:** Disappears before viewers can read
‚ùå **Jarring cuts:** Abrupt transitions without fades
‚ùå **Inconsistent volume:** Some parts too quiet, others too loud
‚ùå **Wrong aspect ratio:** Black bars on sides
‚ùå **Low resolution:** Pixelated or blurry
‚ùå **File too large:** Takes forever to upload
‚ùå **Forgetting end card:** No call-to-action

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Video won't export
**Solution:** Try lower quality settings, close other apps, restart editor

**Problem:** Audio out of sync
**Solution:** Re-import video, ensure frame rate matches original

**Problem:** File too large to upload
**Solution:** Reduce bitrate, use H.264 codec, compress with Handbrake

**Problem:** Video looks pixelated
**Solution:** Increase bitrate, ensure source was 1080p

**Problem:** Can't add text overlays
**Solution:** Use simpler editor or add text in post with online tool
